# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

IT and Operations Admins tracking and assigning corporate assets across the company. They access it through a browser on desktop and tablet devices.

## Product Purpose

To centralize enterprise asset and resource management by tracking the complete lifecycle of physical assets, preventing allocation and booking conflicts, streamlining maintenance and audit workflows, and providing real-time operational visibility.

**Vision:** Simplify and digitize how organizations track, allocate, and maintain their physical assets and shared resources through a centralized ERP platform. This applies to any organization with equipment, furniture, vehicles, or shared spaces (offices, schools, hospitals, factories, agencies). 

**Scope:** The platform aims to reduce manual tracking inefficiencies (spreadsheets, paper logs) by enabling structured asset lifecycles, centralized resource booking, and real-time visibility into who holds what, where it is, and its condition. AssetFlow focuses on delivering core ERP functionality with clean architecture, role-based workflows, and scalable module design **without** touching purchasing, invoicing, or accounting concerns.

## Positioning

AssetFlow unifies asset lifecycle management, resource booking, maintenance approvals, audits, and role-based workflows in one centralized enterprise platform.

## Brand Personality

Reliable, intuitive, efficient. The design should feel like Linear's clean interface combined with Notion's organization and Odoo's enterprise workflows, emphasizing clarity, speed, and usability over visual complexity.

## Anti-references

Avoid cluttered legacy ERP interfaces, excessive forms, overwhelming tables, and outdated gray-heavy designs. The interface should not feel like an old SAP/Oracle application or a generic admin template.

## Design Principles

- **Clarity over density:** Prioritize whitespace and task-focused workflows rather than overwhelming the user with dense screens of data.
- **Speed and usability:** Interfaces should be highly efficient, taking inspiration from consumer-grade tools (Linear, Notion) to make enterprise workflows fast and intuitive.
- **Modern enterprise:** Bring approachability and clean aesthetics to heavy workflows, eschewing the legacy gray-heavy boilerplate feel.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Standard web accessibility (clear contrast, keyboard navigation). No specific strict WCAG compliance required right now.
